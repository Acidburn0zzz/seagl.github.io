Your Conference. Be There.

Save the date

The 2013 Seattle GNU/Linux Conference is scheduled for October 11th and 12th at Seattle Central Community College.

Information on conference registration, schedule will be posted here soon.

Session Topics / Call for Papers

We are looking for anyone interested in running a session dealing with any topic related to Linux and Open Source software in general. If you would like host a session, please send an email to participate@seagl.org with your proposal for a 50 minute presentation, along with any reference material about your qualifications, availablility for Friday or Saturday, and whether or not you will need access to an overhead projector.

Exhibitors and Sponsorshop

We will have a small hall available for commercial exhibitors/sponsors but are still in the process of figuring out how much space will be available and what fees (if any) will be requested. If your organization would like an exhibit table at SeaGL, or would just like to offer financial support, please send an email to sponsor@seagl.org.

Open Source and Educational organizations will be given priority for exhibitor space.
